Kafunk

ConsumerOffsetOutOfRangeAction

Namespace: Kafunk

The action to take when the consumer attempts to fetch an offset which is out of range. This can happen if a) messages are produced faster than they are consumed or b) a lagging broker becomes the leader for a partition. The default action is to halt consumption.

Union Cases

Union CaseDescription
HaltConsumer
Signature:

Halt the consumer, raising an exception.
HaltPartition
Signature:

Halt the consumption of only the out of range partition.
ResumeConsumerWithFreshInitialFetchTime
Signature:

Request a fresh set of offsets and resume consumption from the time configured as the initial fetch time for the consumer (earliest, or latest). If the attempted offset is greater than the latest offset of the topic, it will resume from the latest offset.
